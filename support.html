⚡What Is This About?

The Satoshi Faucet is a labor of love—a modern revival of the original Bitcoin Faucet that helped introduce early adopters to Bitcoin. It's not a money-making machine; it's a tribute to Bitcoin's roots and a fun way to introduce newcomers to satoshis and Lightning Network.

Running a faucet takes resources: server hosting, maintenance, electricity, and time. If you've enjoyed collecting a few sats here, or you simply believe in keeping the spirit of Bitcoin distribution alive, community support helps keep this faucet up and running.

Every bit of support helps. Whether it's a few sats via Lightning or a larger contribution on-chain, your support means we can keep sharing Bitcoin with the world.

🌱How Your Support Helps Bitcoin Grow

When you help keep this faucet alive, you're investing in Bitcoin adoption and education:

  • Accessibility: Faucets introduce new people to Bitcoin and Lightning Network with zero barrier to entry. No KYC, no minimum. Just claim some sats and learn.
  • Lightning Network adoption: Every claim strengthens the Lightning Network as more people discover how fast and cheap Bitcoin transactions can be.
  • Education & community: This faucet comes with resources, documentation, and friendly reminders that Bitcoin is peer-to-peer, open, and trustless.
  • Nostalgia & history: We're preserving the spirit of the original faucet—a piece of Bitcoin history that inspired millions to say "I own Bitcoin" for the first time.
  • Open-source inspiration: This project is built on open principles. Supporting it encourages others to build more community tools and faucets.

🪙A Bit About Bitcoin

If you're new to Bitcoin, here's what you should know:

  • Peer-to-peer digital cash: Bitcoin lets you send value directly to anyone, anywhere, without a middleman. No bank, no government, no corporation in the middle.
  • Scarce and secure: Only 21 million bitcoins will ever exist. The network is secured by millions of computers (nodes) running the same rules.
  • Open-source and trustless: The code is public. You can verify it. You don't need to trust us, a company, or a government—you trust the math and the network.
  • Lightning Network: Built on top of Bitcoin, Lightning enables near-instant, near-free transactions. This is how faucets like ours can send you sats instantly.
  • Self-custody: Your Bitcoin is yours. Keep your own keys, keep your own coins. That's the Bitcoin promise.

Want to learn more? Check out bitcoin.org, the Bitcoin GitHub repo, or explore the Lightning Network.
